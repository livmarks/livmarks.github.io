Michael Reeves 

Michael Reeves is an American Youtuber, Twitch Streamer, and core member of the online social entertainment group, Offline TV.

Born and raised in Maui, Hawaii, Reeves dropped out of university and taught himself how to code and engineer anything he desired. This skill grew so quickly he even secured a job as a software contractor for the U.S government. His online content surrounds his engineering ability and his chaotic nature, creating machines:

  • A “roomba that screams when it bumps into things”
  • A “laser baby” that sets things on fire with it's eyes
  • He even created his own “hand-operated surgery robot”

Michael doesn’t appear to be threatening at face value, but you would be wildy mistaken, as he is incredibly agile, fearless, and strong. Once you step into the ring with “the almighty cracchead” you’re going to wish you never did!
