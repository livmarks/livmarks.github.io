Captain Sparklez 

Jordan Maron, known as CaptainSparklez, is an American YouTuber, streamer, electronic musician and founder of Quality Content clothing line.

Jordan is primarily known for his Minecraft content which he has been creating since 2010. He was the 5th content creator in the world to reach 1 billion video views in 2013.

Maron still uploads minecraft gaming content and is collaborating with new gen creators like TommyInnit and Wilbur Soot for charity events each month.

CaptainSparklez is not shy in showing the world his passion for fitness and frequently shares workout routines and updates on his social media.

The Captain has an unfathomable charismatic attitude that’s matched with a godly strength! He may not want to hurt a fly but you wouldn't want to step in the ring with this OG!
