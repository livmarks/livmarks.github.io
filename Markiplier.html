Markiplier 

Mark Fischbach, better known online as Markiplier, is an American Youtuber originating from Honolulu, Hawaii. He is one of the most popular and well-known content creators in the world. Mark has achieved some incredible feats and has entertained his audience for a mass of 10 years.

He is:

  • A co-founder of clothing company ‘Cloak’ with fellow YouTuber, Jacksepticeye,
  • a co-host for the Distractible Podcast alongside LordMinion777 and Muyskerm.

Markiplier isn’t shy to share his fitness journey and strength. He did athletic based challenges, on the defunct channel Unus Annus with co-host, CrankGameplays.

Markiplier’s muscles are almost as big as his ego. His perseverance and philosophy of always aiming to reach your true potential makes him a difficult opponent to battle…
